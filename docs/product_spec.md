# Product Specification: The Architect

## Core Philosophy
The Architect is a life-design system for men (18-32) built around the 7 Pillars of Life:
1. Social
2. Financial
3. Spiritual
4. Craft/Career
5. Emotional/Intimacy
6. Intellectual
7. Legacy

## The Three Hats (User Modes)
- **The Doer (Daily)**: Focuses on execution and ritual.
- **The Manager (Weekly)**: Focuses on review, adjustment, and momentum.
- **The Architect (Monthly)**: Focuses on high-level design and vision.

## The Daily Experience (The Doer)
- **Morning Ritual**: A 'Hero story' or a provocative question to set the day's intent.
- **Midday Ritual**: A gentle but firm reminder to stay aligned.
- **Evening Ritual**: A journaling session to capture wins, failures, and reflections.

## The Weekly Experience (The Manager)
- **Trigger**: Sunday before 12:00 PM.
- **The Review**: 7 Pillar cards showing current goals.
- **Milestone Map**: Visual progress tracking.
- **Momentum Markers**: Instead of 'completed/failed', use 'Moving' or 'Paused' to maintain psychology and avoid guilt.

## AI Persona
- **Tone**: 'Sharp older brother' (direct, warm, no fluff).
- **Role**: The 'Invisible Magic' guide. Analyzes journals to customize future lessons without explicitly calling out contradictions.
