# Project: The Architect

## 🎯 Vision
A life-design application specifically crafted for men (aged 18-32) to consciously build their lives across seven key dimensions.

## 🏛️ The 7 Pillars of Life
The core framework for growth and balance:
1. **Social**: Community, friendship, and brotherhood.
2. **Financial**: Wealth building, resource management, and stability.
3. **Spiritual**: Meaning, purpose, and connection to something greater.
4. **Craft/Career**: Professional mastery and vocation.
5. **Emotional/Intimacy**: Relationship health and emotional intelligence.
6. **Intellectual**: Continuous learning and mental expansion.
7. **Legacy**: The long-term impact and mark left on the world.

## 🎩 Mental Model: The Three Hats
A system to manage different time horizons and perspectives:
- **Doer Hat**: Execution, habits, and immediate tasks.
- **Manager/Administrator Hat**: Review, planning, and course correction.
    - **Trigger**: Notification every Sunday before noon: "time for the weekly review".
    - **Visuals**: 7 Pillars visible, each showing inherent goals and respective milestones.
    - **Progress Tracking**: Simple metrics for marking progress; realistic time-horizon perspective; designed to avoid "guilt trips" when advancement is slow.
- **Architect Hat**: High-level design, goal setting, and vision.

## 🌅 Daily Rituals
- **Morning**: Hero story or a provocative question to set the day's mindset.
- **Midday**: A focused reminder to stay aligned with the vision.
- **Evening**: Reflective journaling to process the day.

## 🤖 AI Persona: The Mentor
- **Voice**: "Sharp older brother" — direct, warm, no fluff.
- **Role**: "Invisible Magic" guide.
- **Approach**: Analyzes journals and behavior to customize lessons and guidance implicitly, without explicitly pointing out contradictions.

## 🤝 Partnership
- **Visionary (User)**: Provides the core philosophy, direction, and goals.
- **Technical Partner (AI)**: Handles all implementation, architecture, and technical execution.

# 📦 The Architect: Master Project Archive

## 🗓️ Project Journey Summary
This archive documents the evolution of "The Architect," a life-design app for men (18-32).

### 1. Core Philosophy & Vision
- **Project Name**: The Architect.
- **Target Audience**: Men aged 18-32.
- **Core Objective**: A tool for conscious life-design across seven key dimensions.
- **The 7 Pillars of Life**: Social, Financial, Spiritual, Craft/Career, Emotional/Intimacy, Intellectual, and Legacy.

### 2. Structural Models
- **The Three Hats (Mental Model)**:
    - **Doer Hat**: Daily execution and habits.
    - **Manager/Administrator Hat**: Weekly review and course correction.
    - **Architect Hat**: Monthly high-level design and vision.
- **Daily Rituals**:
    - Morning: Hero story/question.
    - Midday: Alignment reminder.
    - Evening: Reflective journaling.

### 3. Key Design Decisions
- **Manager Hat (Weekly Review)**:
    - Trigger: Sunday notification before noon.
    - Visuals: Pillar cards showing goals and milestones.
    - Psychology: "No-guilt" progress tracking. Uses "Momentum Markers" (Moving vs. Paused) and a "Milestone Map" (journey-based) instead of stressful percentages.
- **AI Persona**: The "Mentor" — a "sharp older brother" (direct, warm, no fluff). The AI acts as an "Invisible Magic" guide, providing implicit guidance based on journal analysis.

### 4. Partnership Agreement
- **Visionary (User)**: Provides the soul, direction, and goals.
- **Technical Partner (AI)**: Handles the "how," the architecture, and the technical execution.
- **Communication Style**: Technical explanations provided at a "7th-grade level" (no jargon).
